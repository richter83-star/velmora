OP-ED

From the Editor's Desk

An unsigned confession

VELMORA is fiction, and we'd like to keep it that way. The nation, its parties, its preening would-be rulers, and every scandal printed on this page are invented whole cloth. We satirize the mechanism of power — how it is won, kept, and lost — never any real movement, faith, place, or living person.

It costs nothing. It runs in your browser. It keeps your data on your device, asks for no account, and works on an airplane. There are no trackers in the ink.

Every climb ends in a verdict, and the verdict is rarely kind. That is the joke, and also the point: the throne does not reward you, it reveals you. Now go and ruin a fictional country, responsibly.

— The Editor, who has asked, for reasons of self-preservation, to remain anonymous.
