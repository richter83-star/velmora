Choose your road to power

How does a nobody become
the most powerful person alive?

Velmora is one land with many machines for making rulers. Two roads are open to everyone; three darker ones form The Dark Mirrors. Pick the system you will climb.

The Ballot Path · Democracy

Win the People

Raise money. Work the cameras. Survive the debates. Power is given by voters — and taken away just as fast. Lose an election and your career is over.

Senator→ Governor→ President
The Vanguard Path · One-Party State

Climb the Party

There are no elections that matter. Loyalty, fear, and patronage decide everything. Build a network, out-scheme your rivals — and never let the Party suspect you. Fall, and you are purged.

Cadre→ Regional Secretary→ General Secretary
The Dark Mirrors Expansion Pack · Three Darker Roads
The Iron Order · Strongman

Seize the State

Parliament has failed and the people want order. Build a paramilitary core, control the story, and remove rivals rather than defeat them. Push too hard and the courts, the generals, or the movement itself will end you.

Movement Leader→ Chief of State→ Supreme Leader
The Gilded Republic · Plutocracy

Buy the Republic

The government isn't your enemy — it's your portfolio. Outspend every rival, own the favors, and acquire each check on your power. Overextend, and a prosecutor with nothing to lose — or a richer rival — takes it all.

Board Member→ Chairman→ The Architect
The Anointed Path · Theocracy

Claim the Mandate

The faithful need a shepherd; the Council needs a successor. Grow the congregation, control the doctrine, and be anointed — no vote, just the laying on of hands. Stray from the doctrine and you are cast out.

Parish Elder→ High Prelate→ Supreme Shepherd
